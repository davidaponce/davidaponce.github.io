About me

Hello and welcome :) My names David Poncé, and I'm a Colombian-American Computer Science student at Binghamton University focused on applied AI and data engineering. I enjoy building end-to-end systems, from data pipelines and dashboards to applied AI prototypes like emotion-aware chatbots

My experience spans hackathon-winning prototypes, research, and production-style projects. I’ve worked across Python, FastAPI, Postgres, dbt, Docker, and CI/CD to create practical, industry-aligned solutions. Recent highlights include EcoPrompt (HackMIT 3rd Place, Infosys Diamond Sponsor Challenge), a Chrome extension that reveals AI’s hidden environmental costs, and Reddit Tech Trends, a data pipeline and dashboard surfacing emerging topics in technology communities.

Beyond the classroom, I serve as Makerspace Technical Director, captain of the Division I swim team here at Binghamton, and founded StackHacks, a student-led org for collaborative coding projects. I’m driven by the belief that technology should not only advance capability but also create lasting, responsible impact.
